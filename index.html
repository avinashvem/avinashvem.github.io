Bio

I am currently at Texas A M University pursuing my Ph.D in Electrical and Computer Engineering. I am fortunte to have Dr. Krishna Narayanan as my advisor. Prior to joining Texas A M University in 2011, I received my Bachelor of Technology in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Madras, Chennai, India in 2010. From 2010-11 I worked as an associate engineer at Caterpillar India. During the summer of 2015 I did a summer internship at Intel, Santa Clara in the wireless team. In my free time I regularly follow and play football, or soccer as referred here, and occassionally cricket and tennis too.
